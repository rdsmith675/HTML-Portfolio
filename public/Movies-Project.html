The 3 Greatest Movies of all time According to Richard

My top 3 movies in no particular order

SuperBad

A hilarious coming-of-age tale of two friends looking to lose their virginity before the end of summer. The two friends then realize that when each of them gets into different colleges their biggest fear is losing each other

Scott Pilgrim vs The World

Scott pilgrim finds the girl of his dreams -Literally- and before making it official he must defeat her seven evil exes to win her heart in this video game-inspired action flick

Nacho Libre

A catholic priest played by jack black lives his dream of becoming a masked wrestler but he must avoid being found out otherwise he will be removed from the church
